## üîç Project Overview
This project focuses on building interactive Tableau dashboards to help stakeholders such as sales managers and executives analyze sales performance and customer behavior. The dashboards provide clear year-over-year (YoY) comparisons, highlight key trends, and enable data-driven decision-making through intuitive visualizations, filters, and dashboard actions.

---

## üß† Business Problem
Stakeholders needed a centralized and interactive view to:

- Track overall sales performance and trends over time  
- Compare current year vs previous year (CY vs PY) metrics  
- Identify top-performing and underperforming products and customers  
- Understand customer behavior and order distribution  
- Drill down quickly by product and geographic location

### 1Ô∏è‚É£ Sales Dashboard
**Purpose:**  
To analyze year-over-year sales performance and identify trends across time, product categories, and profitability.

  - Sales
  - Profit
  - Number of orders

### 3Ô∏è‚É£ Interactivity & Design
The dashboards were designed with usability and business clarity in mind:

- Dynamic year selection for historical analysis
- Interactive charts that act as filters
- Navigation between Sales and Customer dashboards

- Consistent layout using Tableau containers
- Custom color logic to highlight:
  - Profit vs loss
  - YoY increase vs decrease
  - Highest vs lowest periods
